Our Story

Family-owned since 2014

My mom opened Aura with two chairs and a dream. She'd been doing nails in Little Saigon for fifteen years before she moved us to LA. I grew up sweeping this floor, watching her freehand roses on acrylic tips at midnight.

Now we have six stations, three full-time artists, and a waitlist most Saturdays. We still do things her way — every set is a conversation, not an assembly line. You tell us what you're feeling, we make it happen. No rush, no shortcuts.
